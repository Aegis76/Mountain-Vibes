Himalayan Legacy & Local Mastery

Forged in the heart of the Chamoli peaks, we represent decades of technical expertise and unshakeable local knowledge. At Mountain Vibes, safety is not just a protocol—it is our heritage.

Meet Our Experts

Mr. Santosh Singh Danu

Founder & Lead Guide

A master of high-altitude navigation with over two decades of technical experience. Certified by the ABVIMAS in both Basic and Advanced Mountaineering, Santosh transitioned from shaping adventure programs in Southern India to leading elite Himalayan expeditions. His leadership is defined by a rigorous commitment to safety and a deep, ancestral connection to the peaks of Uttarakhand.

Pradeep Singh Bisht

Executive Expedition Chef

With 15 years of high-altitude culinary management, Pradeep is an expert in expedition nutrition and hygiene protocols. Trained at the Nehru Institute of Mountaineering (NIM), he mastered the logistics of remote kitchen operations on iconic routes like Roopkund and the Kashmir Great Lakes. He ensures peak physical performance through precision-planned nutrition in the most demanding environments.

Mahendra Singh Danu

Head of Operations & Logistics

The operational architect of Mountain Vibes, Mahendra has been a veteran of the mountains since 1993. His expertise spans advanced technical equipment management, complex logistical planning, and high-altitude rescue operations. As the team’s most senior authority, he provides the surgical precision required to manage expeditions in the unpredictable Himalayan terrain.
