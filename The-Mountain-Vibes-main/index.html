We recently completed a memorable 4-day trek (27–30 April 2026) from Lohajung to Bekaltal, Brahmatal, Jhandi Top, and Daldun with a group of six (three couples). The entire itinerary was flawlessly organised by Mountain Vibes, led by Shri Santosh Singh Danu and his excellent team—Pradeep, Jayveer, and Shri N. S. Rana. The arrangements throughout were outstanding. The lodging had clean linen and was very comfortable. The tents were beautiful, hygienic, and equipped with clean sleeping bags—perfect after long trekking days. Food deserves special mention—simply excellent! Every day featured a different menu. Mornings began with tea and light snacks, followed by wholesome breakfasts including porridge, oats, parathas, fruits, and eggs. Lunches and dinners were equally elaborate, with a variety of nutritious and delicious dishes including dal, vegetables, paneer, rice, rotis, raita, chutney, pickles, papad, soups, and even desserts. Evening tea with snacks added a lovely touch. What truly stood out was the warmth and professionalism of the staff. They were extremely courteous, attentive, and knowledgeable—not only about the local flora and fauna but also rich in mythological insights, which added depth to the entire experience. Overall, it was a wonderfully organised trek with great attention to detail, comfort, and hospitality. Highly recommended for anyone looking to explore the Himalayas with a reliable and caring team. 🙏 Thank you, Mountain Vibes team, for an unforgettable experience! My best wishes to all of you.
